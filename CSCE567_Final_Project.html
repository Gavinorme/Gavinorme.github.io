Introduction

The purpose of this project is to explore player and team performance in the 2024–2025 League One season using interactive data visualizations. By leveraging Tableau Public and statistical data from League One, this project analyzes key performance indicators such as goals, assists, starts, minutes played, and disciplinary records (yellow and red cards). Through these visualizations, we aim to uncover patterns across teams and player nationalities, and highlight trends that impact team success and individual achievements.


Goals

This visualization shows goal statistics by teams, players, and nation. Search bars on the right hand side are provided to compare goals by these categories. England leads the league in goals scored as expected since most players are from England. It also shows the comparison of goals by the amount of minutes played which shows that more goals usually come with more minutes/experience.


Assists

This visualization shows assist statistics broken down by teams, players, and nationalities. Search bars on the right-hand side allow you to filter and compare assist totals across different categories. The data highlights that players with more starts and playing time generally accumulate more assists.


Other Stats (Starts, ranks, cards, etc.)

This visualization explores additional player statistics such as starts, ranks, minutes played, and disciplinary records (yellow and red cards). Filters on the right-hand side allow comparisons between players and teams based on these categories. The data reveals that players with the highest number of starts tend to rank highly in overall minutes played and contributions to their teams, while disciplinary statistics show varying levels of aggression across different squads and positions.


Conclusion

The visualizations clearly show that player performance, especially in goals and assists, is heavily influenced by minutes played and player experience. England dominates the goal charts due to the high proportion of English players in League One. Additionally, team discipline, shown through yellow and red card statistics, plays a crucial role in squad stability. Overall, the data reveals that consistent starters tend to have higher contributions to their teams’ success, and there is a strong relationship between playing time and productivity across the league.
